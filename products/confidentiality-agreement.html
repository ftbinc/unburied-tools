Confidentiality & Safety Agreement

*For participants in peer support groups facilitated by [Organization Name]*

Our Commitment

We create a space where people can speak honestly about their experiences without fear of judgment, exposure, or harm. This agreement protects everyone in the group.

Confidentiality

**Everything shared in this group stays in this group.** This includes identities, stories, and any personal information disclosed during sessions.

**No screenshots, recordings, or notes that identify others.** You may take personal notes for yourself, but do not record audio, video, or photographs of any session unless every participant has given explicit written consent.

**Outside the group** — do not discuss other members' stories, even without names. Context can identify someone.

Exceptions (required by law and ethics)

We may need to break confidentiality if:

  • Someone is at **immediate risk of serious harm** (to themselves or others)
  • There is **suspected abuse of a child, elder, or vulnerable adult**
  • We receive a **valid legal order** (subpoena or court order)

If we must break confidentiality, we will tell you first whenever possible.

Safety Guidelines

**No fixing.** Peer support is not therapy or advice-giving. Listen, reflect, and share your own experience if it helps.

**Share what you're comfortable with.** You never have to disclose more than you want to.

**One person speaks at a time.** No interrupting, no cross-talk.

**Avoid identifying details when sharing.** It's okay to say "someone in my life" instead of naming a person.

**No solicitation.** Do not promote businesses, services, or personal causes during group time.

**Substance-free.** Please attend sessions without the influence of alcohol or non-prescribed substances.

Between Sessions

  • You may exchange contact information with other members if everyone agrees.
  • Group facilitators are available during sessions only. If you need support between sessions, please reach out to your personal support network or a crisis line.

Acknowledgment

By participating in this group, I agree to uphold these confidentiality and safety guidelines. I understand that violating confidentiality may result in removal from the group.
