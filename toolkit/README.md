# Peer Support Operations Toolkit

### Templates, checklists, and guides for peer-led support groups.

---

## Contents

1. **Starting a Peer Support Group** (checklist)
2. **Intake Form Template** — gather what you need from new participants
3. **Confidentiality & Safety Agreement** — protect everyone
4. **Session Note Template** — capture what matters, leave out what doesn't
5. **Outcome Tracking Worksheet** — show the impact of your work
6. **Facilitator Self-Care Guide** — avoid burnout

---

## How to use

Each document is a standalone template. Print them, fill them digitally, or adapt them to your process. They're based on real peer support facilitation — not theory.
